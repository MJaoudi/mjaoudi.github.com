Projects

FencingRef

FencingRef is an iPhone app designed to help Fencing referees judge a bout. Referees would normally use a printed out scoresheet and make points and penalties on there. They would also to have a separate device to keep track of the time. This app was designed to provide a streamlined and efficient way for referees to run and record fencing matches. FencingRef was the first app I ever created. It was originally created in my later years of High School.
